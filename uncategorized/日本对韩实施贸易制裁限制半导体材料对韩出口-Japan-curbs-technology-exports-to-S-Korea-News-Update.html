日本对韩实施贸易制裁限制半导体材料对韩出口 Japan curbs technology exports to S Korea News Update

发表于 2019-08-05 | 更新于 2019-08-17

Japan/South Korea’s Actions

Japan to tighten export rules for high-tech materials to S Korea

  • May expand the list of restricted items to include electronic components and other materials (Japantoday 07-01 )

  • Claims that not in violation of WTO rules (Reuters, 07-02)

  • Uses trade restrictions as a weapon against South Korea (Los Angeles Times 07-21)

  • No plan to retract or renegotiate to South Korea (ABCNew 07-09)

  • Remove South Korea from its “white list” (NIKKEI 0802)

South Korea’s semiconductor and panel industry are facing a very tight situation

  • To file suit with WTO against Japan’s export restrictions(XinhuaNet 07-01)

  • Warns of ‘corresponding measures’ against Japan’s export curbs(TheKoreaTimes 07-04)

  • Hold talks with Japan but ended without progress. ( The Korea Herald 07-12)

  • With no end in sight to the spat, the trade tensions could lead to higher prices for customers globally. (South China Morning Post 07-18)

Other Parties are involved

  • Russia is offering to supply etching gas to South Korea. (Nikkei Asian Review 07-12)

  • The U.S. has no plans to mediate a spat between Japan and South Korea(JapanTimes 07-14), but Trump offers to help if both sides want him. (Reuters, 07-20)

Samsung/Hynix’’s Actions

Samsung is seeking opportunities by all means.

  • Samsung boss visits Tokyo to begin meetings with business leaders there to discuss the issue, also met with SoftBank founder Masayoshi Son. (ZDNET 07-08)

  • Secures emergency supply of key materials from Japan, while it is unknown how he made it or how much the stock is. （The Straits Times 07-14）

  • Tests non-Japan materials(hydrogen fluoride) from China mainland, Taiwan and local, and may take 2-3 months. (Nikkei Asian Review 07-17)

  • Asks partners to stockpile Japanese components (Reuters 07-18)

  • Seeks to diversify smartphone display, battery suppliers like Kolon Industries and SKC (the Investor 07-21)

SK Hynix is preparing for worse future

  • SK Hynix warns of chip supply disruption on Japan’s export curbs（Reuter 07-25）
